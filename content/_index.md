Five years at AWS Professional Services, where I led $30M+ in complex architecture engagements — from monolith decomposition and production RAG systems to agentic AI for clinical development in GxP-regulated environments.

I've architected genomics pipelines that scaled to 1,000+ concurrent samples, built ML-powered manufacturing inspection platforms that cut defect rates by 80%, and delivered GenAI reporting portals serving hundreds of enterprise customers in production. My work spans healthcare, life sciences, financial services, HR technology, and media — always at the intersection of deep technical execution and strategic business alignment.
